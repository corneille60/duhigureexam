Getting Started Guide

Follow these simple steps to begin your family's journey

Begin by accessing the system through the link provided below. You have two options:

  • Enter your family data independently if you're comfortable with technology
  • Seek assistance from an Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga (technology facilitator) or your local administrative authority

Create your family profile by entering essential information for each household member:

  • Full names and relationship to household head
  • Ages, education levels, and occupations
  • Individual roles and responsibilities within the family

Work together as a family to establish meaningful goals:

  • Set individual performance duties for each family member
  • Create shared family objectives (Imihigo) that require collaboration
  • Define clear, measurable targets and deadlines

Keep your family on track with regular monitoring:

  • Update achievements and milestones as they're completed
  • Review progress dashboards together during family meetings
  • Celebrate successes and adjust goals as needed
